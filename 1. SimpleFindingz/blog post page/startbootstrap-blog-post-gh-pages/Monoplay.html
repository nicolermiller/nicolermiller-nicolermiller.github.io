Instant Winners

by Simple Findingz

Posted on May 9,2017

When you see those Monopoly are being given to you at the register and you declined them, think again. Yeah, you may not be the one in a million to win that dream home or the grand prize but there are some other cool perks.
I've collected hundred's of these tickets. You can recieve them by purchasing certain items at Albertson's, Vons and other various participating stores. Check their website for full details.
Some people,m myself included get excited when you recieve a bunch of these small tickets even when purchasing very few items. ( I've won serveal instant win to get more free tickets). You can also win free grocery items in stores like Bakery donuts/bagels, bread, baby carrots, string cheese. You can use serveral coupons at once.
There's an coupon app called SnipSnap. You can search instant win and coupons will appear. I was even able to use these for free items!
Act fast though, the promotion ends May 9th and coupons expire May 30th.
Hey, for those that do win the grand prizes.. Congratulations!
