Top Extensions

by Simple Findingz

Posted on April 4, 2017

Who doesn't like someone doing the work for you?

Listed below are a few Chrome friendly extensions along with others used on other browsers including Firefox,Safari,Opera. Chrome seems to be a popular browser for most users.
Pocket (Chrome/Firefox/Safari/Opera/Edge) Able to save all your bookmarks on your tool bar to easily get to your favorite pages later on.
1Password – A great concept came along- this website &extensions store & save your passwords.. It's a great idea to have a secure place for your inmportant information.. Only downside is that is cost $2.99/monthly or $4.99 which includes up to 5 family members that are added to the account where they can share and save their passwords.
Social Media
Pin it (Chrome)- Simply click to share you favorite pins right from your tool bar.
Instagram- View & share content on your account
Travel
Invisible Hand (Chrome, Firefox and Safari) Finds the best price for items your going to buy. Traveling? This can find the lowest price when you search for flights, hotels and rental cars.
Shopping
Honey (Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Opera)
Ebates (Chrome) I love to be able to click on the E button in my tool bar, to access ebates and seeing what cash back offers are at the stores I'm going to make a purchase at.
Coupons At Checkout(Chrome, Safari, and Firefox) Displays & beats deals automatically
Graphics
Color Picker – Can identify the precise color of anything in your browser
